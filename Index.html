Pixal Bangla Studio

Our mission: To fill the gap for high-quality Bangla games, showcase unique ideas, and bring Bangladeshi gaming culture to the world stage.

About the Studio:

Pixal Bangla Studio is an independent game development studio, where I single-handedly handle every aspect of game creation. From conceptualizing ideas, designing, coding, creating graphics and sound, to testing and releasing—every step is done personally.

The Studio’s Main Goals are to:

  • Create unique, smooth, and engaging experiences for players.
  • Incorporate Bangla language, culture, characters, and folklore into games.
  • Showcase the capabilities of Bangladeshi developers globally.

Ultimately, the goal is not just to make games, but to create experiences that delight players, keep them engaged, and bring them back for more. Pixal Bangla Studio – Crafting games for Bangladesh, powered by one person’s passion.
